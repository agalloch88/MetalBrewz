About

MetalBrewz spawned from the COVID19 quarantine morass as a creative outlet. With no metal shows or festivals to attend, and breweries nationwide closed, or only doing cans and bottles to-go, the project launched to keep these underground arts brimming with interest.

Whether you love beer, metal, or both, hopefully you will find something new and intriguin in our archives. If you have brews or tunes you want to send, hit us up below!
